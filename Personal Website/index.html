Welcome to my personal website! My name is Yujie (Elena) Pu, and I am currently a Lecturer/Instructor of Imperial Chinese History in the Department of East Asian Languages and Cultures at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My research interests include Social and Cultural History of Late Imperial China, Global History of Medicine and Psychiatry, Legal History, Intersection of Medicine and Religion, and Manchu Texts.
